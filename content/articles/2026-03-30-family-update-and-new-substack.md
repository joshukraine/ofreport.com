## A Long Winter in Ukraine

…Missiles and drones launched by russia wreaked havoc on electricity
substations, leaving thousands of people without electricity, heating, or hot
water for days at a time. Kyiv declared an energy crisis and asked as many
people as could to leave. The capital, and many other eastern and southern
regions, are experiencing outages to date, and many homes are still without
heat.

Here in western Ukraine, our family endured a few tense moments and many, many
hours without electricity. Late on January 8th, the sirens went off, and we
began making our way to the hall for shelter. No sooner had we gathered the kids
together than we started hearing explosions…
